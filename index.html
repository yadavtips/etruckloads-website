About Us

India's Most Trusted & Leading Logistic Service Provider

We at Etruckload logistics pvt ltd serve from last more than 10 years in road transportation sector in india to fulfill our clients needs. To serve our best in class services we use various type of commercial vehicles like tata chota haathi, mahindra pickup, tata 407, eicher 1109, 20, 22 and 32 feet close body container trucks and 20 to 60 feet long trailers. We etruckload logistics use our tempo, dala body, container trucks and trailers to move all type of industrial, commercial, household goods from all metro cities to all over india.

From 2011 we focus mainly on 32 feet single axle (7 to 9 ton), double axle (14 to 17 ton) and triple axle (20 ton to 24 ton) container body vehicles to ensure safe and secured delivery of delicate items for various industries like electronics, furniture, snacks, automobile, packaging, pharma, export material and many more.

We give service guarantee on each shipment because of our well trained field staff, drivers and technology equipped (GPS enabled) fleet.

So please contact us without any doubt in our services.
